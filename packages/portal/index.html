咖啡冲煮的通用语言 The Universal Language for Coffee Brewing

一粒种子，为咖啡世界建立数字秩序 A seed that brings digital order to the world of coffee

BrewCode OS 是一个开源项目，为全球咖啡冲煮建立通用数字语言。核心资产是一个名为 .brew 的 JSON 标准文件格式。

诞生记

一粒种子落下来了。2026年，一个自由人面对三台电脑，问了一个问题：咖啡的终极数字形态是什么？答案不是App，不是平台——是一个标准。一个让全世界的咖啡师、烘焙师、爱好者都能说同一门语言的标准。

A seed has fallen. In 2026, a free individual sat before three computers and asked: what is the ultimate digital form of coffee? The answer was not an app, not a platform—it was a standard. A language that baristas, roasters, and enthusiasts across the world could all speak.

咖啡是物理，不是玄学。任何可描述的风味差异，必然对应可测量的参数差异。研磨度、水温、粉水比——这些数字不是冰冷的，它们是风味的密码。当你学会了这门语言，一杯咖啡就从神秘变成了精确。

Coffee is physics, not mysticism. Every describable flavor difference corresponds to a measurable parameter difference. Grind size, water temperature, brew ratio—these numbers are not cold. They are the cipher of flavor. Learn this language, and a cup of coffee transforms from mystery into precision.

冲煮知识是公共财产。没有人应该因为不知道"正确的"参数而喝不到好咖啡。所以我们把 .brew 标准以 CC0 协议发布——永久免费，永久开源，属于全人类。这不是一个产品，这是一个运动。

Brewing knowledge is a public good. No one should be denied a great cup of coffee because they don't know the "right" parameters. That is why we release the .brew standard under CC0—permanently free, permanently open, belonging to all of humanity. This is not a product. This is a movement.
